About

What is PPE detection?

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) detection is a technological solution designed to enhance workplace safety by automatically identifying instances of non-compliance with safety regulations regarding the use of Personal Protective Equipment. This technology utilizes cameras and sophisticated deep-learning algorithms to analyze real-time footage or images captured within work environments, such as construction sites, mines, manufacturing facilities, healthcare settings, and more.

What are the benefits of PPE detection?

Fully automated

It is almost impossible to check whether workers are wearing hard hats and other PPE at all times simply using human labor. Automated PPE detection through AI cameras and video analytics is a perfect solution in a multitude of settings.

Timely notifications

As soon as a hard hat or any other PPE is removed by a worker, a customizable alarm will immediately sound and the security personnel will receive an instant notification. This minimizes the time a worker would be in danger.

Easy to implement

Taking advantage of industry-leading AI algorithms, the technology brings accurate PPE detection to a selection of hardware – from video cameras to access control equipment – so as to ensure worker safety in hazardous environments.
